Lasagna

Get ready to make some Lasagna, quick & easy!

Ingredients

  • Riccota
  • Sour Cream
  • Ground Meat
  • Lasagna Pasta Box
  • Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • Basil, oregan, garlic powder, onion powder, salt/pepper
  • Ceramic Pan

Cooking Steps

  1. Step 1: In a pot heat up water mixed with salt & olive oil till boiling.
  2. Step 2: Once water is boiling pour lasagna pasta into pot and cook until aldente.
  3. Step 3: In a separate bot heat up extra virgin olive oil just enough to cover the bottom.
  4. Step 4: Once the oil is hot to feel add the ground meat and cook until its evenly cooked. Stir occasionally.
  5. Step 5: Add a bit of basil, oregano, garlic, onion, pepper, salt, and mix throughly. Cook until the meat turns brown.
  6. Step 6: Lower stove temperature to low heat and pour rague sauce and mix. Let it boil then turn off.
  7. Step 7: Grab a ceramic pan and lay 4 of the lasagna pasta. Pour meat enough to cover the pasta and make a bottom layer.
  8. Step 8: Add small gobs of sour cream & riccota.
  9. Step 9: Add another 4 layers and repeat steps 7-8 until satisfactory with the amount of layers.
  10. Step 10: On the last layer add three cheeses and preheat oven to 350F. Then put pan into oven and let cook for 40-45 mins.
  11. Step 11: Serve & enjoy!!
