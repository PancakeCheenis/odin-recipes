Long Island Iced Tea Recipe

Ingredients

  • Vodka
  • Tequlia
  • Triple Sec
  • Gin
  • Rum
  • Sweet & Sour
  • Coke

Mixing Steps

  1. Step 1: Mix ice, vodka, tequila, triple sec, gin, rum, Sweet & Sour in a mixer.
  2. Step 2: Pour mixture into cup with ice.
  3. Step 3: Add coke to liking and stir slowly.
  4. Step 4: Enjoy!
