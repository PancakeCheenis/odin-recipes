Long Island Iced Tea Recipe

This recipe requires a bit of ingredients, but its well worth. Fair warning it'll taste like a lemonade & tea mixture but will leave you knocked out in just a couple of drinks.

Ingredients

  • Vodka
  • Tequlia
  • Triple Sec
  • Gin
  • Rum
  • Sweet & Sour
  • Coke
  • Mixer Cup
  • Strainer

Mixing Steps

  1. Put ice into a cup of your choice for the cocktail inorder to chill the cup. Never chill the liquor always the cup!!
  2. Grab the mixer cup put ice in one half and pour 1.5oz of each alcohol into the other half. Add the sweet & sour mixture with the alcohol to your liking.
  3. Combine the two halfs of the mixer and shake vigorously for 1 minute.
  4. Set the mixer aside. Dump the cup with the ice in it and with a strainer covering the mixer cup pour into the drinking cup.
  5. Pour coca-cola to your liking and stir slowly.
  6. Enjoy!
