Jungle Juice Recipe

Ingredient List

  • Blue Raspberry Lemonday Kool-Aide Packets
  • Silver Tequila
  • Coconut Rum
  • Everclear
  • Lemonade
  • Squirt Soda
  • Blue Curacao
  • Orange Slices
  • Lemon Slices
  • Raspberries

Steps

  1. Pour all the alcohol into a 10 gallon container.
  2. Added the Kool-Aide packets & mix.
  3. Add lemonade & soda & mix.
  4. Add Orange, lemon, & raspberries.
  5. Pour & Enjoy!
